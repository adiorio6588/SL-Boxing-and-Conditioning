ELIZABETH SEXTON

Fitness Specialist

Yoga began its steady transformation in Elizabeth's life when she was in high school. At first, it was just the physical practice the ballerina fell in love with. Today, Elizabeth still loves stretching and strengthening her body through the physical practice of yoga, but with an added appreciation for yogic philosophy. She's constantly inspired by Yoga’s integrative use of empowerment, health, healing, and self-discovery.

As a 2010 graduate of Chicago's first-ever PURE Yoga Teacher Training at Equinox, Elizabeth fell deeper into her practice under the leadership of her Chicago teacher trainer "gurus" - Forrest Yoga rockstar, Allison English and Illumined's Lourdes Paredes. Inspired by their compassionate instruction, Elizabeth began to develop a teaching yoga style that celebrates the cultivation of heart-healthy optimism and the embodiment of bliss.

Elizabeth believes in teaching for ANYONE and EVERYONE, and she passionately enjoys experiencing new styles of yoga and forms of sound therapy. Her classes are accessible, creative, and challenging for all-levels. Elizabeth thrives when teaching to diverse groups, from stretching out the athletic teams at the University of Chicago Lab School to leading rooftop yoga classes at the Aqua building. When she's not on the yoga mat, Elizabeth's innovative side gets a workout in marketing, social media and PR. She currently oversees marketing programs at Chaturanga Holistic Fitness and is also the Director of Fitness and Digital Media for Aligned Modern Health, where she has been teaching yoga for more than 5 years.
